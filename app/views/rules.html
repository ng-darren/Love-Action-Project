Our Rules

Love Action Project seeks to provide a platform for

  1. individuals to embark on social/charity work,
  2. individuals to showcase their talent and leadership,
  3. individuals to socialize and network with other like-minded individuals, because we believe people with a good heart deserves to know another.

We welcome and support projects in the diverse categories of Art, Animals, Children, Elders or in fact Everyone but there are three rules every Love Action Project project must follow.


Projects are a one time event that has a altruism aim.

A project is a finite event with a clear charitable goal that you would like to bring to life. Think carnivals, celebrations, or concerts for orphanages and elders home. Or if you have some brilliantly creative ideas, and you are not sure if we will support it, contact us.

Projects must be honest and clearly presented.

Our community is built on trust and communication. Projects cannot mislead people or misrepresent facts, and creators should be candid about what they plan to accomplish. When a project involves something complex, we require project creators to show the commitment and know-how to realise that project.

Projects can’t offer financial incentives.

We’re all in favor of charity and investment, but projects can’t offer financial incentives like allowances and salary to the volunteers. We also do not allow any of these prohibited things in the projects:

  • Offer coupons, gambling, raffles, and lifetime memberships
  • Contain offensive material (e.g., hate speech, encouraging violence against others, etc)
  • Offering or marketing financial, money-processing, or credit services; financial intermediaries or cash-equivalent instruments; travel services (e.g., vacation packages) and business marketing services
  • Affliate to any political messages
  • Contain any pornographic material
  • Contain any tobacco, drugs, and drug paraphernalia

If your project involves something illegal, heavily regulated, potentially dangerous or even if you have a slightest hint of doubt, please contact us before starting your project.



These rules don’t cover every possible project in Love Action Project, but they explain our purpose and perspective. If you have any questions, feel free to get in touch.
