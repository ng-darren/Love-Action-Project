What is Love Action Project?

Love Action Project vision is threefold.

We seek to provide a platform for

  1. individuals to embark on social/charity work,
  2. individuals to showcase their talent and leadership,
  3. individuals to socialize and network with other like-minded individuals, because we believe people with a good heart deserves to know another.

We advocate the concept of “having fun but never forget to give”. As much as we provide an avenue for individuals to give, we also encourage them to step forward to showcase their talents, and develop their leadership skills with us. In addition, we also create a platform for everyone to socialize and network at the end of the day. Our concept of charity is not just one that provides an avenue to give, but rather, we wish to plant the seeds of charity in our volunteers’ hearts, so that they can reap the benefits of giving, and pay it forward to the society.

In time to come, Love Action Project will look to pursue a much more interesting venture: Charity Overseas. Where we seek to create a unique overseas experience for Volunteers as we travel to countries around our region to for charity work. We wish to not only give volunteers time to give, but also time to interact with each other, meet new faces and make new friends and allow like-minded people to hang out and share the ideas.

Our Beliefs

Here in Love Action Project, we believe in the equal and inherent value of all human and animal life. We believe that a worldview bound by borders is outdated and that helping people and animals anywhere is the responsibility of humanity everywhere.

How does Love Action Project work?

Our team is constantly on the lookout for meaningful projects that fits the vision of our movement. Each project is independently created and crafted by the Love Action Project Team together with the supporting agencies/charity organisations. Our unique value proposition is that, even as we collaborate, we seek to have complete control and responsibility over the projects. This is to allow us to give opportunities for our volunteers to come in and influence the running of the projects, to enhance their involvement and experience of giving. Our volunteers will spend time to provide inputs or even be involved in our planning process weeks before an event, and see through the successful completion of it, knowing that they have been a major contributor to the success of it.

Every project by Love Action Project requires sponsors. At such, we welcome organisations that wish to embark on charity work to join us.

Who should support Love Action Project?

Everyone! Bookmark our Website or Like our Facebook page, and visit us regularly to check if we have any upcoming projects!

Profile of a Project Leader: Individuals who have passion, innovative ideas and a genuine desire to improve the society and environment, and wish to embark on the challenge to run the whole project by him/herself, with the support from the Love Action Project Team.  As a project leader, you can be from an organization or even an individual who wish to leverage on Love Action Project to give. Basically, you are the one that leads, and make the project a reality, with us supporting you at the back.

 

Profile of a Team leader: Individuals who wish to be part of the planning team for a Love Action Project event.  As a team leader, you will lead a certain portion of the event (e.g. Entertainment, Games Management, Children Management, etc.) with the help from the Love Action Project Team. You will join us in brainstorming, planning and the eventual execution the event. Your involvement goes beyond just giving, you lead a team under you, and you make sure they have a pleasurable giving experience like you do.

Profile of a Volunteer: Individuals who are outgoing and fun-loving, and who are able to contribute their time and expertise . As a volunteer, you choose the project you wish to be involved in, and the area you wish to contribute to. You become part of a social community and play a crucial part in making things happen.

So support our ongoing projects or volunteer to be part of Love Action Project.
