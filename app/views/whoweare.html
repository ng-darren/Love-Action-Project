Our Team

We are a team of volunteers who dream about empowering Singaporeans to Spread Love, Give Hope and Take Action.




Joe

"The CEO"

Joe Tan is a soldier by day, and an aspiring writer and philosopher by night. Joe believes that who he is today, is very much the reflection of how society believes in him, and the opportunity that these people had granted him. And its exactly because of these, that he finds the purpose of creating Love Action Project, to give others the opportunities that he was so privileged to be bestowed with.
